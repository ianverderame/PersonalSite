About me

Hello, and thank you for coming to my website! My name is Ian Verderame, and on October of 2021 I began my career as a web developer. I have recently completed the Skill Distillery bootcamp where I learned Java, JavaScript, MySQL, OOP, RESTful APIs, HTML, CSS, Spring Boot, Angular, and more (I highly highly recommend Skill Distillery, and if you have any questions about it, please feel free to reach out to me!). If you ask me and absolutely nobody else, I graduated top of the class.

As a fullstack developer, I work on both front and back end of website, though I find myself leaning more towards the backend. I consider myself a lifelong learner, and will continue to learn new programming languages and concepts.

Since 2015, I have been a certified EMT-IV, first in California, now in Colorado. I have worked on the ambulance, in a Level II trauma center ER, and for two years I taught an EMT class in San Jose, CA. I've loved my jobs, but I'm ready to transition out of healthcare and into the world of software/web development, though I keep all of my certifications up to date and still pick up the occasional weekend.

Currently, I am based out of Denver, Colorado, with my wife, Charli, and our cat, Napkin. I have also lived in San Jose, California, and was raised in a small town in New Hampshire. I have always had a passion for helping people, and can't wait to do this through coding meaningful programs. I am also a hardcore Arsenal fan, so if you ever want to talk about soccer, I'm your guy!
